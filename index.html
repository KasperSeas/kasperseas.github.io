The Process

Most of the projects are done with agile project management and teamwork.

Scrumban

Scrumban is a combination of the team managements methodologies Scrum and Kanban.

During Scrumban we plan weekly sprints. We perform "stand ups" to demo what we've worked on for that week and present any blockers that hinder our performance to help team synchrony. We focus our work by creating individualized project paths. We iterate continuously until our goals are met. And in the end we review ourselves, what worked best for us and what can we improve on.
