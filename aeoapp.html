Aeoapp

Aeoapp is an application that finds you a like minded work-out buddy around you!

Aeoapp was the first ever iOS application I worked on. I worked with a team of 6, 3 marketers and 3 developers including myself at a hackathon by Google's Startup Weekend. Starting from scratch, us 3 developers worked 17 hours non-stop to create the first implementation of Aeoapp. Pictured above is us 3 developers after our 17 hack session, still pumped to showcase what we created.
